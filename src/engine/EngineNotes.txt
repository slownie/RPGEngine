Stats Array:
0 = HP
1 = MP
2 = ATK
3 = MAG
4 = SPD
5 = DEF
6 = RES

Skill Types:
0 = Physical Damage (Uses HP, ATK, and DEF stats)
1 = Magical  Damage (Uses MP, MAG, and RES stats)
2 = Healing         (Uses MP, and MAG stat)
3 = Status Ailment  (Will eventually use the SKL and LCK stats)
4 = Buff            (Will eventually use the SKL and LCK stats)
5 = Debuff          (Will eventually use the SKL and LCK stats)
